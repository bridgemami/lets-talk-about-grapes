Let's Talk About Grapes

The Why

I want to create a site to inform people about grape varietals that are not as well known. For this project, I choose six grape varietals that have great importance in the history of wine,  are grown in well-known wine regions and are varietals I enjoy personally.

Hopefully the viewer learns something new about wine and the growing regions.

Varietals

  • Albariño
  • Cabernet Franc
  • Carménère
  • Chenin Blanc
  • Pinot Meunier
  • Sémillon

The Who

The creator of this site is Michael Bridgeman. I am a student at Santa Rosa Junior College. I am trying to earn a Web Full-Stack Developer AS. This site was created for the final project for CS 50A. I have worked in a wine tasting room environment for 13 years. Other than Albariño, I have not served these varietals. Hopefully, after visiting the site, you will try one of these varietals.
